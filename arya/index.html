Welcome to "Treasure Hunt on the web". Unlike any other treasure hunt, this one is based on word guessing.


How to play:

1. You’ll be given hints to find a word/group of words which will help you to find the next hint.
2. The word/pair of word will be used in the web URL to find the next hint.
3. The following part of the URL will stay same for the entire game.

https://rahuldf.github.io/arya/

(You can copy this for your reference)


4. There are a total of 6 word/pair of words to find out. (all lower-case)




Here’s an example of the URL

Suppose your guessed word is “hello”
Your URL will be: https://rahuldf.github.io/arya/hello

suppose your guessed word is “hello world”
Your URL will be: https://rahuldf.github.io/arya/hello-world
